\subsection*{Description\-: }

Routine to add hydrogens to a protein linked list of type P\-D\-B. The routine allocates space for the new atoms and inserts them into the list at the appropriate positions within the residues.

N.\-B. Because of the variation in styles required for N-\/terminal Hs, this routine adds {\itshape no} hydrogens to the N-\/terminal residue. Fix\-P\-D\-B() may be used for this purpose if C\-H\-A\-R\-M\-M/\-C\-O\-N\-G\-E\-N style hydrogens are required.
